What Is Forex Trading?

Forex trading involves buying and selling currencies in a global marketplace with the aim of making a profit. It's the largest financial market in the world, open 24 hours a day, 5 days a week.

Whether you're a beginner or experienced, you can participate by using leverage to trade currencies, commodities, and other assets. Learning the ins and outs of Forex trading can lead to substantial profits, but it requires knowledge, strategy, and discipline.
